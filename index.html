About Me

A software engineer based in new york city.

I am a web developer who loves solving problems with technology and building things with code, particularly products and tools that empower people's lives. My journey began with a career in the hospitality industry, but I decided to pursue software development because it allowed me to express my creative side and hone my technical abilities. As a graduate of Fullstack Academy's Grace Hopper Program, I have gained a strong foundation in web development and transitioned into a versatile skillset. I'm ready and excited to find opportunities that will continuously help me grow while working alongside likeminded professionals.

Fun Fact: When I am not coding, You can find me hiking or adventuring out around nature.
